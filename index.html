WHAT WE DO

Professional mobile bar hire with full insurance, licensing, and the service of our experienced staff.

Our Service Packages

Package 1

The Cash Bar

We organise everything - the licence, staff, bar units, serving equipment, glassware, and drinks. Your guests pay for drinks as they require them, just as if they were in a pub or bar.

Cash bars do not accept cheques or credit cards

Package 2

The Credit Bar

We organise everything as for Package 1, but you pay for the drinks your guests consume. Pre-payment required with balance settled on the night. Hourly statements available.

Popular option: Mix of both packages (e.g., £200 behind the bar to start)

We find that 90% of people choose one of the above packages or a mixture of the two.

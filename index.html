WHAT WE DO

Professional mobile bar hire with full insurance, licensing, and the service of our experienced staff.

Our quality, standards and professionalism are the very best you can get. We work with a small close-knit team so you are always guaranteed the highest levels of customer service and a very personal approach. Rest assured your event is our main priority and we will ensure a stress-free, totally reliable and professional service for you.

All of our bespoke bars can be individually designed and include ice wells, bottle coolers, draught fonts, cask ale fonts and speed rails - all the features you would require for delivering a professional and unrivalled drinks service.

Our Service Packages

PACKAGE 1

The Cash Bar

We organise everything - the licence, staff, bar units, serving equipment, glassware, and drinks. Your guests pay for drinks as they require them, just as if they were in a pub or bar.

💰 Cash payments only

PACKAGE 2

The Credit Bar

We organise everything as for Package 1, but you pay for the drinks your guests consume. Pre-payment required with balance settled on the night.

🔥 Popular: £200 to start, then cash

PACKAGE 3

Dry Hire Bar

Hire all the professional equipment to provide your own service. We can help you with Bar Staff and a purchasing drinks package if required.

🛠️ Perfect for self-managed events

PACKAGE 4

Wedding Drinks

Complete wedding drinks package including reception drinks, wedding toast bubbly, half bottle of wine per person for breakfast, plus unlimited teas & coffees.

💍 Includes FREE Cash Bar

Choose the package that best suits your event, or we can create a custom combination to meet your specific needs.

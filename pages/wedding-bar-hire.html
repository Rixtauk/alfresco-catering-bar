Your Perfect Wedding Day Deserves the Perfect Bar

We understand that your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. That's why we're here to ensure every toast, every celebration, and every magical moment is accompanied by perfectly crafted drinks and impeccable service.

From the moment your guests arrive for the welcome drinks to the final toast of the evening, Alfresco Catering Bar will be there to create an unforgettable experience that you and your loved ones will treasure forever.

How We Make Your Wedding Dreams Come True

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your vision, guest count, preferences, and venue requirements. This is where we start planning your perfect bar experience.

2

Licensing & Setup

We handle all the licensing requirements and coordinate with your venue. Our team takes care of everything so you can focus on your special day.

3

Perfect Service

On your wedding day, our professional team arrives early, sets up your beautiful bar, and provides exceptional service throughout your celebration.

Our Wedding Bar Packages

PACKAGE 1

The Cash Bar

We organise everything - the licence, staff, bar units, serving equipment, glassware, and drinks. Your guests pay for drinks as they require them, just as if they were in a pub or bar.

💰 Cash payments only

PACKAGE 2

The Credit Bar

We organise everything as for Package 1, but you pay for the drinks your guests consume. Pre-payment required with balance settled on the night.

🔥 Popular: £200 to start, then cash

PACKAGE 3

Dry Hire Bar

Hire all the professional equipment to provide your own service. We can help you with Bar Staff and a purchasing drinks package if required.

🛠️ Perfect for self-managed events

PACKAGE 4

Wedding Drinks

Complete wedding drinks package including reception drinks, wedding toast bubbly, half bottle of wine per person for breakfast, plus unlimited teas & coffees.

💍 Includes FREE Cash Bar

Choose the package that best suits your wedding, or we can create a custom combination to meet your specific needs.

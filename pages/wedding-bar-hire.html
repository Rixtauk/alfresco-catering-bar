Your Perfect Wedding Day Deserves the Perfect Bar

We understand that your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. That's why we're here to ensure every toast, every celebration, and every magical moment is accompanied by perfectly crafted drinks and impeccable service.

From the moment your guests arrive for the welcome drinks to the final toast of the evening, Alfresco Catering Bar will be there to create an unforgettable experience that you and your loved ones will treasure forever.

How We Make Your Wedding Dreams Come True

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your vision, guest count, preferences, and venue requirements. This is where we start planning your perfect bar experience.

2

Licensing & Setup

We handle all the licensing requirements and coordinate with your venue. Our team takes care of everything so you can focus on your special day.

3

Perfect Service

On your wedding day, our professional team arrives early, sets up your beautiful bar, and provides exceptional service throughout your celebration.

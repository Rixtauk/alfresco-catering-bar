Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a licence for our venue?

You need not worry, Alfresco Catering will apply to obtain the necessary licences for you.

How much notice do you need for a licensed bar?

To ensure a successful application, we require a minimum of 4 weeks. This is to gather all required information for your event that a court may ask for. Any less than 4 weeks may not guarantee the granting of your licence.

Are outside bars expensive to purchase drinks from?

We strive to keep our prices extremely competitive. We work closely with a range of suppliers to keep our prices reasonable.

How will you keep our drinks cool in summer?

Alfresco Catering will supply all draught products chilled with our chilling equipment, and ice will also be available for other drinks.

How much space is required for an outside bar?

The minimum set-up area is 10ft x 10ft (3m x 3m) for a full bar set-up. We also require a 16A socket within 20m and a supply of tap water nearby.

What drinks do you supply?

We only use branded products and can supply almost any drink you can think of. We can supply lists of commonly requested beers, wines, spirits and soft drinks, but any particular request can usually be supplied.

What do we get with a basic set-up?

We will apply to the courts for your licence including court fees and you get a fully stocked bar, ice, glassware and our fully trained staff. We take licensing law very seriously as it is illegal to sell alcohol to anyone under the age of 18.
